PROCESS

I believe keeping the process of your project simple is the best way forward. This not only makes things less complicated, but also saves time. Here are the steps we'll take to get your project completed:

1. TALK:

We'll schedule a free call via Google Meet/Zoom/phone (or email) to discuss your project goals and problems. I'll write up a proposal with a summary of our discussion, what you'll receive, costs, and the time frame.


2. DESIGN & DEVELOP:

I'll draft up a web design (or other design) that meets your satisfaction. The web design will be developed using WordPress (other design made with different software). Your site will be tested on all devices to ensure responsiveness.

If we're only working on a logo or another design, then this is the last step! The design will be given to you after your review and approval.


3. LAUNCH:

After your review and approval of your website, we'll schedule a launch date! Launch typically takes 2 business days. A Google Business profile can be set up at this time (if needed).


4. POST:

Regular maintenance is required; I can maintain and update your site as needed. I'm able to make changes, modifications, etc. to manage your website and Google Business profile.
