About Me

Hey, I'm Miguel Lozano.

I'm a freelance web designer

based in Muskegon, MI.

Nice to meet you!

I grew up enjoying video games, building things with Lego pieces, and tinkering with computers and other technologies. Eventually, I learned basic HTML and CSS in high school as well as AutoCAD. I ended up going the architectural route in college and became a structural draftsman for seven years.

I left my career in late 2021 and attended Muskegon Community College in 2022. After taking various web and graphic design related courses, I graduated with an Associate in Web Design and earned my Responsive Web Design certificate from freeCodeCamp.

Aside from working with marketing agencies as an independent contractor, I create clean and professional websites and iconic logos for individuals, small and medium-sized businesses. I will utilize WordPress or static HTML to help make your business grow. I love helping those that are starting out or are in need of a redesign. If you are in need of a website, logo, another project, or all of the above, then let’s get in touch and make it happen.
